SaveTheBite

Track what you own. Eat within budget. Reduce food waste. Get rewarded.

The Problem

Students overspend on food while throwing away perfectly edible groceries. Expiry dates are forgotten, budgets are exceeded, and sustainability goals feel abstract.

Our Solution

Smart Food Tracking

Track what food you already own using receipt scans or quick inputs. Get reminders before items expire.

Budget-Friendly Meal Planning

Generate meal plans and recipes using only existing ingredients while staying within your budget.

Gamified Sustainability

Join challenges, reduce waste, and earn credits for positive food behavior.

How It Works

  1. Set food preferences, budget, and sustainability goals
  2. Track food inventory and receive expiry alerts
  3. Generate affordable meal plans and grocery lists
  4. Complete challenges and earn credits
  5. Redeem credits at campus canteens, grocery stores, and cafés

Business Model

University Partnerships

Universities pay for student wellbeing, sustainability impact, and ESG reporting.

Retail & Café Partners

Grocery stores and cafés gain footfall through credit-based redemptions and promotions.

Sponsored Challenges

Brands sponsor challenges aligned with affordability and sustainability.
