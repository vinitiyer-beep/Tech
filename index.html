SaveTheBite

A smart food and budgeting app for students that helps you eat better, spend less, reduce food waste, and earn real rewards.

The Problem

Students overspend on groceries while throwing away food. Sustainability feels important, but unrewarded.

Food Waste

Groceries expire unnoticed and end up in the bin.

Budget Stress

Monthly food expenses are hard to track and control.

No Incentives

Doing the right thing doesn’t feel rewarding.

Our Solution

SaveTheBite turns smart food behavior into savings and rewards.

Smart Inventory

Track what you own and get notified before food expires.

Budget-Based Meals

Generate meals and recipes using only available ingredients.

Gamified Rewards

Earn credits for saving food and staying within budget.

How It Works

Set preferences, budget, and goals
Track groceries and expiry dates
Get recipes and meal plans
Complete challenges
Earn and redeem credits on campus

Business Model

Universities

Pay for student wellbeing, ESG impact, and food waste reduction.

Retail & Cafés

Gain footfall and incremental revenue through redemptions.

Sponsors
